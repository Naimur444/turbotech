Product Design

Our expert team of mechanical engineers specializes in creating innovative and efficient product designs. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and develop solutions that meet both functional and aesthetic requirements.

  • Design for manufacturability (DFM)
  • Prototyping and testing
  • Design optimization

3D Modeling & Simulation

We utilize cutting-edge 3D modeling and simulation tools to bring your ideas to life and optimize designs before production.

  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Motion and kinematic analysis

Structural Analysis

Our structural analysis services ensure the integrity and safety of your mechanical designs under various loading conditions.

  • Static and dynamic analysis
  • Fatigue and fracture mechanics
  • Thermal stress analysis
  • Vibration analysis and modal testing
